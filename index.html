About Us

At the intersection of Computer Science and Social Science we conduct applied research into intelligent data intensive systems and human-centered explainable AI and their application in social media, technology-enhanced learning, and knowledge management domains. We offer courses at both bachelor and master levels in the areas of data science, learning technologies, web technologies, visual analytics, and human-AI interaction.
